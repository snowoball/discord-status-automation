Statuses

Manage your statuses. A status is a message that can be displayed on your profile. You can use placeholder macros which will be dynamically replaced based on location and date/time from where this service is run.
Supported macros include:
{{weather_emoji}}
{{weather_text}}
{{time_emoji}}
{{time_text}}
{{timestamp_text}}
You can organize your statuses using tags so they are easier to find in the presets.
